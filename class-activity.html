How to make Simple Rice!

Ingredients:

  • Rice; 1/2 Cups
  • Water 1 Cup
  • Salt (To Taste)
  • Vegetable (To Taste)

Fun fact: Ratio of rice to water is 1:2, means we use twice the quantity of water as we use of rice.

Instructions:

  1. Take 1/2 cups of rice and rinse them.
  2. Take a pan and pour 1 cup water in it. Set heat to max.
  3. Add rice to pan with water and add salt according to taste.
  4. Cover it for 10 minutes and keep check to not burn rice.
  5. Turn off the heat.
  6. Cover the pan for approx. 5 minutes.
  7. When no visible water at bottom, your rice are ready to be served.
